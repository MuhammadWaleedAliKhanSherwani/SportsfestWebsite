Sports Categories

Detailed information about all available sports in the tournament

Futsal

5-a-side indoor football with fast-paced action

Team Composition

  • 5 players on field (4 outfield + 1 goalkeeper)
  • Maximum 7 substitutes allowed
  • Minimum 3 players to start match

Match Duration

  • 2 halves of 20 minutes each
  • 5-minute half-time break
  • No injury time added

Rules & Regulations

  • FIFA futsal rules apply
  • No offside rule
  • 4-second rule for restarts
  • Unlimited substitutions

Basketball

3x3 street basketball tournament

Team Composition

  • 3 players on court
  • 1 substitute allowed
  • Minimum 2 players to continue

Match Duration

  • 10 minutes or first to 21 points
  • 2-minute overtime if tied
  • Shot clock: 12 seconds

Scoring System

  • 1 point for free throws
  • 1 point for shots inside arc
  • 2 points for shots outside arc

Table Tennis

Individual and doubles table tennis

Categories

  • Men's Singles
  • Women's Singles
  • Men's Doubles
  • Women's Doubles
  • Mixed Doubles

Match Format

  • Best of 5 games
  • First to 11 points per game
  • Must win by 2 points
  • Best of 7 for finals

Rules

  • ITTF rules apply
  • Serve changes every 2 points
  • No coaching during play
  • Let serves are replayed

Arm Wrestling

Strength competition with weight categories

Weight Categories

  • Lightweight: Under 70kg
  • Middleweight: 70-85kg
  • Heavyweight: 85-100kg
  • Super Heavyweight: Over 100kg

Match Format

  • Best of 3 rounds
  • 30-second rest between rounds
  • Double elimination bracket
  • Winner advances to next round

Rules

  • Both elbows must stay on table
  • No lifting elbow off table
  • Hands must be clasped
  • No false starts allowed

Athletics

Track and field events including sprints and relays

Track Events

  • 100m Sprint
  • 200m Sprint
  • 400m Sprint
  • 4x100m Relay
  • 4x400m Relay

Field Events

  • Long Jump
  • High Jump
  • Shot Put
  • Discus Throw
  • Javelin Throw

Rules

  • IAAF rules apply
  • False starts result in disqualification
  • Relay baton must be passed in zone
  • No assistance from coaches

Swimming

Freestyle, breaststroke, and relay competitions

Individual Events

  • 50m Freestyle
  • 100m Freestyle
  • 50m Breaststroke
  • 100m Breaststroke
  • 50m Backstroke

Relay Events

  • 4x50m Freestyle Relay
  • 4x50m Medley Relay
  • Mixed 4x50m Freestyle
  • Mixed 4x50m Medley

Rules

  • FINA rules apply
  • No false starts allowed
  • Proper stroke technique required
  • Touch pad timing

Badminton

Fast-paced racket sport with singles and doubles

Categories

  • Men's Singles
  • Women's Singles
  • Men's Doubles
  • Women's Doubles
  • Mixed Doubles

Match Format

  • Best of 3 games
  • First to 21 points per game
  • Must win by 2 points
  • Maximum 30 points per game

Rules

  • BWF rules apply
  • Serve must be underhand
  • No coaching during play
  • Shuttle must land in bounds

Volleyball

Team sport with 6 players per side

Team Composition

  • 6 players on court
  • 6 substitutes allowed
  • Libero position available
  • Rotation system required

Match Format

  • Best of 5 sets
  • First to 25 points per set
  • Must win by 2 points
  • 5th set to 15 points

Rules

  • FIVB rules apply
  • Maximum 3 touches per side
  • No double contact
  • Net height: 2.43m (men), 2.24m (women)

Cricket

T20 format with 11 players per team

Team Composition

  • 11 players per team
  • Maximum 4 overs per bowler
  • Wicket-keeper mandatory
  • Substitute fielders allowed

Match Format

  • 20 overs per side
  • Maximum 4 overs per bowler
  • Powerplay: First 6 overs
  • Super over for tied matches

Rules

  • ICC T20 rules apply
  • Wide and no-ball penalties
  • Free hit after no-ball
  • DRS available (if technology present)

Football

11-a-side outdoor football

Team Composition

  • 11 players on field
  • 7 substitutes allowed
  • Minimum 7 players to start
  • Goalkeeper mandatory

Match Format

  • 2 halves of 45 minutes
  • 15-minute half-time break
  • Injury time added
  • Extra time if needed

Rules

  • FIFA rules apply
  • Offside rule in effect
  • No handball except goalkeeper
  • VAR available (if technology present)

Tennis

Individual and doubles tennis

Categories

  • Men's Singles
  • Women's Singles
  • Men's Doubles
  • Women's Doubles
  • Mixed Doubles

Match Format

  • Best of 3 sets
  • First to 6 games per set
  • Must win by 2 games
  • Tie-break at 6-6

Rules

  • ITF rules apply
  • Serve must be overhand
  • Ball must bounce once
  • No coaching during play

Squash

Individual racket sport in enclosed court

Categories

  • Men's Singles
  • Women's Singles
  • Men's Doubles
  • Women's Doubles

Match Format

  • Best of 5 games
  • First to 11 points per game
  • Must win by 2 points
  • Maximum 15 points per game

Rules

  • WSF rules apply
  • Ball must hit front wall
  • No interference allowed
  • Let and stroke decisions

Hockey

Field hockey with 11 players per team

Team Composition

  • 11 players on field
  • 5 substitutes allowed
  • Goalkeeper mandatory
  • Rolling substitutions

Match Format

  • 4 quarters of 15 minutes
  • 2-minute breaks between quarters
  • 10-minute half-time
  • Penalty shootout for ties

Rules

  • FIH rules apply
  • Only flat side of stick
  • No high balls
  • Penalty corners and strokes

Wrestling

Individual combat sport with weight categories

Weight Categories

  • Lightweight: Under 65kg
  • Middleweight: 65-80kg
  • Heavyweight: 80-100kg
  • Super Heavyweight: Over 100kg

Match Format

  • 3 rounds of 2 minutes
  • 30-second rest between rounds
  • Best of 3 rounds
  • Sudden death if tied

Rules

  • UWW rules apply
  • No striking allowed
  • No joint locks
  • Pin for immediate win

Boxing

Individual combat sport with weight categories

Weight Categories

  • Flyweight: Under 52kg
  • Bantamweight: 52-57kg
  • Lightweight: 57-63kg
  • Welterweight: 63-69kg
  • Middleweight: 69-75kg
  • Heavyweight: Over 75kg

Match Format

  • 3 rounds of 3 minutes
  • 1-minute rest between rounds
  • 10-point must system
  • Knockout or decision

Rules

  • AIBA rules apply
  • Headgear mandatory
  • No hitting below belt
  • Referee can stop fight

Karate

Martial art with kata and kumite events

Categories

  • Kata (Forms)
  • Kumite (Sparring)
  • Individual events
  • Team events

Match Format

  • Kata: 3 minutes maximum
  • Kumite: 3 minutes per bout
  • Best of 3 rounds
  • Point system scoring

Rules

  • WKF rules apply
  • Protective gear mandatory
  • No contact to head
  • Respect and discipline

Taekwondo

Korean martial art with kicking techniques

Categories

  • Poomsae (Forms)
  • Kyorugi (Sparring)
  • Individual events
  • Team events

Match Format

  • Poomsae: 60 seconds
  • Kyorugi: 3 rounds of 2 minutes
  • Point system scoring
  • Electronic scoring

Rules

  • WT rules apply
  • Head and body protection
  • Kicking techniques only
  • No punching to head
