About the Tournament

Experience the thrill of professional futsal competition in Pakistan

Elite Competition

Compete against the most skilled futsal teams from across Pakistan. Only the best teams from Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, and other major cities qualify for the championship rounds.

Professional Standards

FIFA-approved futsal rules, certified Pakistani referees, and professional-grade courts ensure fair and competitive gameplay that meets international standards.

Three-Day Event

Intensive tournament format with group stages, quarterfinals, semifinals, and the grand championship final. Experience the best of Pakistani futsal talent.

Tournament Format

  • 90+ teams from across Pakistan divided into groups of 4
  • Round-robin group stage matches
  • Top 2 teams from each group advance
  • Single elimination knockout rounds
  • 20-minute halves for all matches
  • FIFA futsal rules strictly enforced by Pakistani referees
