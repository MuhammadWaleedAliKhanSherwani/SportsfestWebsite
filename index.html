About the Tournament

Experience professional futsal competition in Pakistan

Elite Competition

Compete against the most skilled futsal teams from across Pakistan. Only the best teams qualify for championship rounds.

Professional Standards

FIFA-approved futsal rules, certified Pakistani referees, and professional-grade courts ensure fair competition.

Three-Day Event

Intensive tournament format with group stages, quarterfinals, semifinals, and the grand championship final.

Tournament Format

  • 90+ teams from across Pakistan divided into groups of 4
  • Round-robin group stage matches
  • Top 2 teams from each group advance
  • Single elimination knockout rounds
  • 20-minute halves for all matches
  • FIFA futsal rules strictly enforced by Pakistani referees
