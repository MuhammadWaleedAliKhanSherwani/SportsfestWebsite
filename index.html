Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the tournament

What is the registration fee?

The registration fee depends on the number of sports: Rs 2000 for one sport, Rs 2300 for two sports, and Rs 2500 for three sports. Socials are included in the registration fee. There is also a delegation fee of Rs 3000 for Private Delegation/Institutions.

How do I pay the registration fee?

You can pay through JazzCash (Account: 0332651000, Name: Hassan Ahmad Qureshi) or Bank Transfer (Bank Al Habib, Account: 5501 1865 0163 52503, Name: Fasih Ahmad Qureshi). After making payment, you MUST send screenshots to our WhatsApp (+92 300 1234567) with your team name and captain's contact number. Registration is confirmed only after payment verification.

When is the tournament?

The tournament will take place from December 22-26, 2025 in Lahore, Pakistan at three locations: DPS Model Town Ground (Model Town, Lahore), K21 Padel Court (Lahore), and Aligarh Cricket Ground (Lahore).

What sports are available?

We offer 16 sports categories: Futsal (5 players), Cricket, Basketball (3x3), Throwball, Volleyball, Dodgeball, Badminton, Chess, Ludo, Carrom, Scavenger Hunt, Gaming, Table Tennis, Athletics, Strongmen, and Tug of War. You can participate in multiple sports.

How do I register my team?

Click the "Register" button in the navigation menu to access the registration portal. Create your team account, fill in all required information (team name, members, sports selection), make the payment, and send the payment screenshot to our WhatsApp. You'll receive confirmation within 24 hours after payment verification.

What age groups can participate?

The tournament welcomes Private Delegation/Institutions with categories for Under 17 and Under 22 age groups. All participants must provide valid ID proof during registration.

What is included in the registration?

Registration includes participation in selected sports, 2 social events, safety measures (Police, Bouncers, and armed security), and tournament facilities. All participants will compete in a knockout-based tournament format.

Is accommodation provided?

Accommodation is not provided by the tournament organizers. Teams are responsible for their own accommodation arrangements. We can provide recommendations for nearby hotels upon request.

What safety measures are in place?

We take safety seriously. The event will have Police presence, Bouncers, and armed security personnel on-site throughout the tournament. All participants must follow the Code of Conduct which prohibits alcohol, drugs, and weapons on premises.

How can I contact the organizers?

You can reach us via WhatsApp at +92 300 1234567 (24/7 support), Phone at +92 300 1234567 or +92 301 9876543 (9 AM - 8 PM), or Email at info@pakistanfutsal.com or registration@pakistanfutsal.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
