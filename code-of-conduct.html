Code of Conduct

Rules and guidelines for all participants

Sportsmanship

Respect all players, officials, and spectators. Maintain fair play and positive attitude throughout the tournament.

Safety First

Follow all safety protocols. Use proper equipment and report any injuries immediately to event officials.

Punctuality

Arrive 30 minutes before your scheduled matches. Late arrivals may result in match forfeiture.

Prohibited Items

No alcohol, drugs, or weapons allowed on premises. Violation will result in immediate disqualification.

Disciplinary Actions

Unsportsmanlike conduct may result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent disqualification from the tournament.

Communication

Keep your contact information updated. Event officials will communicate important updates via WhatsApp and email.
